About JuggleFit

JuggleFit was founded to create a sport-juggling competition that's motivating to practice, requires minimal preparation, is fun and enjoyable to watch, ensures the best juggler wins, and measures skill through control over specific tricks.

Inspired by CrossFit's challenge of defining athleticism, we set out to build a fair, exciting, and accessible juggling competition for everyone.

Rules of JuggleFit

Routes

JuggleFit is built around "routes" — sequences of juggling tricks drawn from a list of general skills. A secret route is revealed just minutes before the competition begins.

Competition Format

Competitors race to complete the route, and the first to finish wins. If no one completes the route within 10 minutes, the winner is the one who progresses furthest the fastest.

Qualifications

Competitors must complete a secret list of tricks within 30 minutes. The tricks vary by route (e.g., balls, clubs, rings).

Categories

Routes are divided by props: balls, clubs and rings. Optional categories include Youth (under 16/18) and Women.
